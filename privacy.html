We collect the following types of information:

We use the information we collect to:

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS Mumbai region). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

NEET-AI uses the following third-party services:

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

6. Your Rights
